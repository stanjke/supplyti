About Us

The Ukrainian successor of one of the subsidiaries of the Austrian Rustler Group, which has been successfully operating in the Ukrainian property management market, promoting European values and quality of work. We entered the Ukrainian market in 2022 and have since been successfully providing property management and maintenance services to both foreign and Ukrainian clients. Our priority is optimizing the operation of the managed property, which consequently increases its efficiency and profitability. We provide comprehensive management services for shopping malls and business centers across Ukraine.
